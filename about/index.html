About me

Dokkodo Sounds is his main artistic project, which focuses around Lo-Fi, Ambient and Downtempo. He has releases with labels including Inner Ocean Records, College Music, Pueblo Vista and many more. The project has achieved over 5 million streams and been featured on multiple Apple Music and Spotify editorial playlists including Lo-Fi Beats, Road Trip To Tokyo, BEATstrumentals and Ambient Sleep.

Through his work as Dokkodo Sounds, he has become an established Producer and Sound Designer. Thomas creates content and sample packs for Touch Loops, SoundGhost and Ampify (Novation / Focusrite) iOS Apps Launchpad and Blocs Wave.

Thomas composes Production Music for various music libraries and publishers, including EMI, Ninja Tune Production Music, WotNot (Cavendish), Felt, Apache and Pretty Decent Music.

He has had syncs with Netflix, BBC, Channel 4 and MTV.

Thomas has a deep passion for music education. He lectures at Leeds Conservatoire on the BA Production and FdA Electronic Music Production courses.

Outside of creating or teaching music, you will probably find Thomas drawing on his i-Pad or playing Magic the Gathering.

It should also be noted, Thomas is not a fan of writing biographies in the third person.
